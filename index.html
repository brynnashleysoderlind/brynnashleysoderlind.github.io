About Brynn

Brynn paints, builds mobiles, works with textiles, and renovates properties that most people would walk past. She sees what something could be and makes it real. That's just how she operates.

She's also a mother of two, which — if you know her — explains everything about how she keeps it all together. She doesn't do things halfway.
